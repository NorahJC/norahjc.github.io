Let's Learn

Animals! Animals! And more Animals!

This site is to help little kids learn about animals. It engages kids' interest by incorporating colors and images. The site is also interactive. Kids can hover over images and play sounds.

The animals that are displayed are those that are most common and not too difficult to pronounce. Kids have the opportunity to learn about different animals, the animal's name, and the sound that the animal makes. They also get to look at more animals if they'd like in the gallery!
